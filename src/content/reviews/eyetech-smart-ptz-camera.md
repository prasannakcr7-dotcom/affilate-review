## Our Verdict
The Eyetech 4+4MP Smart WiFi PTZ CCTV Camera is Eyetech's budget-friendly outdoor security camera. It is built for home and shop surveillance and competes directly with similar offerings from Qubo and Guard Well. Overall, it is a good value for property owners wanting 360-degree color coverage on a budget, though it depends entirely on a stable internet connection and encodes video in a proprietary format on the memory card.

## Dual-Lens Performance & Video Quality
The camera features a dual-lens design rated at 4+4MP by the manufacturer. It captures video at 1080p resolution at 25 frames per second. According to customer reviews, the video feed has clear daytime detail and allows users to choose between Standard Definition (SD) and High Definition (HD) streaming modes depending on their bandwidth.

For nighttime monitoring, the unit features built-in infrared (IR) and white LEDs. The manufacturer claims this setup provides full-color night vision up to 30 feet (15 meters). Buyer feedback confirms that the night capture has reasonable clarity in dark conditions, provided there is some ambient light or proper illumination near the installation site.

## PTZ Rotation & Smart Features
The camera includes motor-driven pan, tilt, and zoom (PTZ) functionality. Users can rotate the camera 355° horizontally and 90° vertically to cover a wide monitoring field. According to customer reviews, controlling the rotation via the mobile app is responsive and simple. However, some buyers note that the camera has a blind spot directly above the unit because the tilt mechanism cannot reach the top-side area.

The camera has AI-powered motion tracking and real-time alerts. When the system detects human movement, it sends instant push notifications to the user's mobile device. It also includes two-way audio with a built-in microphone and speaker. Multiple buyers report that the audio connection is clear enough for basic communication with visitors at their entrance or yard.

## Connectivity, App & Encrypted Storage
The device connects via 2.4GHz WiFi and requires a constant internet connection to operate and transmit live feeds. Multiple buyers state that the camera will not function without active WiFi. The camera uses the V380 Pro mobile application, which users report is easy to connect and navigate.

For recording storage, the unit supports Micro SD cards up to 128GB as well as optional cloud storage subscriptions. A notable limitation reported by buyers is that the camera encrypts the files on the memory card. The files cannot be read or played back by inserting the card into another device like a computer or smartphone; playback is only possible through the official mobile app.

## Build Quality & Weatherproofing
Constructed from a white plastic shell, the dome camera is designed for outdoor wall mounting. The manufacturer lists the water resistance rating as IP67, while the listing text states it is an IP66-rated weather-resistant body. Buyers confirm that the housing holds up well to dust and rain at home entrances or balconies.

The package includes a corded electric power adapter, mounting screws, and an instruction manual. Since it is not a battery-powered unit, installation requires access to an electrical outlet. While the plastic casing is lightweight, multiple reviews indicate the mounting bracket is simple to install and holds the camera securely.

## Should You Buy It?
The Eyetech 4+4MP Smart WiFi PTZ CCTV Camera is a good choice for budget shoppers who want a flexible outdoor security camera for a house entrance, balcony, or shop yard. It handles 360-degree pan-tilt coverage and two-way audio well, and its ₹2,079 price is cheaper than most dual-lens options. The main limitations are its reliance on active internet connectivity and the encryption on the SD card files that prevents direct playback on other devices. If those are not concerns for your setup, it is worth considering. If you need a camera that can record offline or has open video encoding, look at the Qubo Smart Cam 360 instead.
