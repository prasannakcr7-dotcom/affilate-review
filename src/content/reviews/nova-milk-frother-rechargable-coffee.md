## Our Verdict
The Nova Rechargeable Milk Frother is the brand's budget-friendly drink mixer. It is built for frothing milk and mixing light beverages, and it competes directly with alternatives from Bonsenkitchen and Lifelong. It offers a convenient, budget-friendly option for home coffee preparation, though its high rotation speed requires a deep container to prevent splashing.

## Frothing Performance & Speeds
According to customer feedback, this handheld frother creates creamy foam in under 20 seconds. The motor spins a stainless steel whisk head to incorporate air into both hot and cold liquids. Buyers report that the motor has enough power to whisk eggs, blend protein shakes, and mix hot chocolate.

The device features three speed settings, which are selected using physical buttons on the handle. While these settings offer versatility, multiple reviews note that the highest speed is very strong. If used in a shallow cup, this rotation speed can cause liquids to overflow or splash. Users recommend starting on the lowest speed setting and using a tall mug to contain the liquid.

## Design & Detachable Whisk
The unit is constructed with an Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) plastic body and weighs 200 grams. This lightweight design makes the handle comfortable to hold during use. 

For cleaning, the stainless steel whisk head can be pulled off the motor shaft. This allows you to wash the whisk attachment under running water without exposing the electrical body to moisture. The plastic housing is not waterproof and should only be wiped clean with a damp cloth. A major limitation of this package is the lack of a storage stand or case, which means the device must be stored in a drawer where the whisk wires can bend. Additionally, some buyers reported that the product packaging they received differed from the images on the Amazon listing page.

## Battery Life & Type-C Charging
Instead of relying on replaceable AAA batteries, the device features a built-in rechargeable battery. The manufacturer claims that a single charge provides up to 30 frothing cycles. Buyer reviews support this claim, stating that the battery lasts for several weeks of daily coffee preparation before requiring a recharge.

Charging is handled via a standard USB Type-C port located at the base of the handle. This allows you to use the same charging cables as modern smartphones. The manufacturer does not state the exact time required for a full charge, but buyers report that the unit charges quickly and maintains consistent power output until the battery is depleted.

## Should You Buy It?
The Nova Rechargeable Milk Frother is a good choice for home coffee drinkers who want café-style foam without paying for an expensive machine. It handles milk frothing quickly, and its USB-C recharging capability is better than most battery-operated options at this price point. The main limitations are the potential for splashing in shallow cups and the absence of a storage stand. If those issues are not concerns for your daily use, it is worth considering. If you need a model that includes a countertop stand, look at the Bonsenkitchen Rechargeable Milk Frother instead.
